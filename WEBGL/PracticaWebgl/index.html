Controls

You can move through the scene with the controls W, A, S, D and with the mouse.

Change color background: "M"(white), "N"(black), "B"(random color);

To activate fog around the model: "F";     To change color of the fog: "C" ;     To activate/desactivate rotation: "R";     To activate/desactivate lights: "L";     To control intensity of lights: "+"/"-";     To control rotation direction mantain: "O"/"P";
